About Me

I am Brandon Cotten, a front-end web developer based in the Fraser Valley, B.C. I'm passionate about creating user-friendly and visually appealing websites that provide great experiences for users. With a strong background in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I enjoy turning ideas into functional web applications. In addition to my technical skills, I love keeping up with the latest design trends and technologies to ensure my work remains modern and effective. When I'm not coding, I enjoy exercising by doing Crossfit and hiking. I also enjoy playing video or board games and reading.
